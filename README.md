##PixelArtCollab!

Made to actually get familiarized with React + Vite + Tailwind. 

In this website you can connect with your friends and paint together. First, you are gonna be asked your name or how do you want the others to see you, then, you can start painting and making drawings together with your fellas, if you have something to say, you can always use the chat, but don't worry, if you made a mistake you can delete your message for everyone.

If the chat is too full you can always delete it locally, the others will still se every message but they won't bother you.

Each time someone enter the page, it will announce it by telling you their name and upping the amount of connected people at the top of the page.

To paint, just choose your ffavourite collor from the palette or find another one using the RGB (by pressing on the larger square), then you can choose to paint square by square using the "brush" button or to fill the whole canvas using the "fill" button, don't worry, you can always erase or clear all if you make any mistakes or you wanna start over
